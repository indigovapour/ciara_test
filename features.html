Featured Artist March 2015

Tom Brooker

Tom works as a painting assistant for one of Britain's most well known artists. He also produces his own artwork and takes on private commissions. Tom's artwork generally focuses on architectural landscapes and portraiture.

"For any artist assigning their work to a gallery, it can be a hard and daunting prospect. I think with Indigo Vapour Art it gives amateur artists an exciting alternative platform to exhibit their artwork to the public"

Tom Brooker
