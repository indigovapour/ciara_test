About Us

Make. Share. Enjoy Art.

Who We Are?

Indigo Vapour Art is a social enterprise. We have created an innovative platform to connect artists and appreciators (clients).



What We Do?

We showcase talented artists work and provide appreciators with access to quality, handmade art-work at a fair price. We offer three services to compliment this platform; art consultancy, delivery and installation.



How is our social enterprise business model sustainable?

Simple. We generate revenue via two streams.

Firstly, through the nominal joining fee for Artists and Appreciators. Secondly, via the services we provide.
The money we generate is re-invested into our business. This helps to cover the operational costs incurred in providing this unique platform.

Future aims: to introduce a third revenue stream by securing public funding. This will enable us to abolish our joining fee.

We also hope to establish and grow a face-to-face community by curating a series of open nights and exhibitions. Of course, absolutely no commission fee will be entertained.
