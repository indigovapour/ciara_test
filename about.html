About Us

Who we are?

Indigo Vapour Art is a social enterprise initiative. We have created an innovative platform to connect artists and appreciators (clients). We believe in nurturing artists and providing appreciators with access to quality handmade art at fair prices.

What we do?

Products and services to conect artists and appreciators.

How we do sustain our business model?

Our principal income source is via two streams:

  1. Personalised services: art consultancy, transportation and installation
  2. A nominal joining fee for Artists and Appreciators
This money is re-invested into our social enterprise and helps cover the operational costs involved in providing this unique platform. In the future we aim to secure public funding to abolish this fee. We also hope to create a face-to-face community via open nights and exhibitions, and absolutley no commission fee's. We believe in quality handmade art at fair prices for artists and appreciators.
